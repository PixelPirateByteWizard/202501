## 关于Gelro

Gelro是一个充满活力的社区平台，我们将分类知识与社区互动完美融合。在这里，每位用户不仅能获取专业的分类指导，还能与志同道合的环保爱好者分享经验、交流心得。

Gelro is a vibrant community platform where we perfectly blend waste sorting knowledge with community interaction. Here, every user can not only access professional sorting guidance but also share experiences and exchange insights with like-minded environmental enthusiasts.

我们相信环保不是孤独的旅程。通过Gelro，您可以记录自己的环保足迹，参与社区挑战，与他人共同成长。每一次正确的分类，每一次知识的分享，都是我们共同守护地球的温暖力量。让我们携手，让环保成为一种生活方式，让绿色成为我们共同的语言。

We believe that environmental protection is not a solitary journey. Through Gelro, you can record your eco-friendly footprint, participate in community challenges, and grow together with others. Every correct sorting, every knowledge sharing, is our collective warm power to protect the Earth. Let's join hands to make environmental protection a lifestyle and green our common language.

Gelro不仅仅是一款应用，更是连接人与自然的桥梁。我们用科技的温度，传递环保的温情。

Gelro is not just an application, but a bridge connecting people and nature. We use the warmth of technology to convey the compassion of environmental protection.
